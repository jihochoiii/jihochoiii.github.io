about me

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University. I am currently working as a research intern at NetZero LAB under the supervision of Dr. Heejin Kim. My research at NetZero LAB mainly focuses on demand response and its applications. Prior to joining NetZero LAB, I worked as an undergraduate researcher at Compiler Optimization Research Lab (CoreLab), advised by Prof. Hanjun Kim. My focus of study at CoreLab was trusted execution environments, strengthening my interest in computer architecture and security.

I am currently looking for a PhD position starting in Fall 2025. My primary research interests are security and parallelism in the following domains: computer architecture, compilers, operating systems, and embedded systems.
